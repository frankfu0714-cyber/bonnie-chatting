### Description
有時候，最難的不是答案，而是開口問自己。

「幫你決定?」收錄了七種輕巧的儀式，把那些卡在心裡的小猶豫——「中午吃什麼？」「該打那通電話嗎？」「要不要剪短頭髮？」——交給一個帶著廟宇氣息的角落來陪你決定。

**七種儀式**

• **筊杯** —— 傳統的擲筊，三種結果：聖筊、笑筊、陰筊。讓木紋與紅漆告訴你天意是什麼。

• **花瓣** —— 一片一片摘下，「會、不會、會、不會……」直到最後一片決定答案。童年的占卜，永遠不會褪色。

• **抽面紙** —— 從紙盒裡輕輕一抽，答案就藏在指尖。

• **求籤** —— 搖籤筒、抽一支竹籤，看看今天的籤詩怎麼說。

• **轉盤** —— 把選項一條條寫上，轉一下，讓命運停在哪裡就是哪裡。最多可放十個選項。

• **銅板** —— 最古老的決定方式。指尖一彈，正面還是反面？

• **隨機數字** —— 在你設的範圍裡，挑一個數。簡單而誠實。

**所有答案，都可以由你自己定義**

每一種儀式都附帶可自訂的選項。是 / 否、做 / 不做、紅 / 藍、A / B——你的選項，就是你的命運。

**廟宇般的氣息**

灰泥牆、丹砂紅、暖金邊、木頭紋理。每一個動作都帶著重量感，沒有彈跳，沒有花俏，只有一份靜謐的安定。中文以宋體呈現，每一個字都像剛從石碑上拓下。

**完全離線、絕對私密**

• 沒有帳號、沒有登入

• 沒有廣告、沒有內購
• 所有資料都只存在你的手機裡

這不是一款想賺你錢的 app。這是一個放在口袋裡的小角落，獻給每一個在小事上猶豫的你。

免費，永遠。

Bonnie Chatting

### Promotional Text (≤170 chars)
Seven gentle rituals to help you decide the small things — moon blocks, petals, tissues, fortune sticks, a wheel, a coin, a number. A temple corner in your pocket.

### Keywords (≤100 chars)
decision,fortune,jiao bei,fortune sticks,wheel,coin,flower,divination,help,choose,ritual,temple

### Description
Sometimes the hardest part isn't the answer — it's letting yourself ask the question.

**Bonnie Chatting** is a tiny app of seven small rituals, gently helping you decide the things that are too small to ask anyone else. "What should I eat?" "Should I make the call?" "Should I cut my hair short?" Slip the question into one of the rituals, and let a quiet corner with a temple aesthetic answer back.

**Seven Rituals**

• **Moon Blocks (筊杯)** — The traditional jiao bei toss. Three outcomes: sheng (yes), xiao (laughing), yin (no). Wood grain, cinnabar lacquer, the weight of an old tradition.

• **Petals** — She loves me, she loves me not. Pluck them one by one until the last one decides.

• **Tissues** — Pull a tissue from the box. Your answer is inside.

• **Fortune Sticks (求籤)** — Shake the bamboo cylinder, draw a stick, read what today's verse has to say.

• **Spinning Wheel** — Write your options around the rim, give it a spin, and let it land where it lands. Up to ten options.

• **Coin Flip** — The oldest way to decide. Heads or tails?

• **Random Number** — Pick a number from a range. Honest and direct.

**Every Answer Is Yours**

Every ritual lets you customize the options. Yes / No, Do / Don't, A / B, red / blue — your options become the outcome.

**A Temple Aesthetic**

Aged paper, deep cinnabar red, warm gold, wood textures. Every animation has weight — no bouncy springs, no flashy effects. Just a quiet, grounded stillness. Chinese text is set in Songti, like rubbings from a stone tablet.

**Completely Offline, Completely Private**

• No accounts, no sign-in
• No cloud, no tracking, no analytics
• No ads, no in-app purchases
• Everything stays on your device

This is not an app trying to monetize you. This is a small corner you can keep in your pocket, made as a gift to anyone who hesitates over the small things.

Free, forever.
